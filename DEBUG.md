# Keys for Debugging
* `M`: Turn to **Menu**
* `G`: Turn to **Game**
* `R`: Change **Team(Color)**
* `T`: Transform **Pos/Map**
* `C`: Clear **Projectile/Effect**
* `V`: Change **Weapon**
* `X`: Change **Sheet/Translations**
* `~`: Transform **Game Speed**
* `Enter`: Enter one **Game Step**
* `Shift E`: Force get **Game Result**
