# Debug Keys:
## <M\>
Trun to **Menu**
## <G\>:
Trun to **Game**
## <R\>:
Change **Team(Color)**
## <T\>:
Transform **Pos/Map**
## <C\>:
Clear **Projectile/Effect**
## <V\>:
Change **Weapon**
## <X\>:
Trun **Sheet/Translations**
## <`~\>:
Transform **Game Speed**
## <Enter\>:
Makes one **Game Ticking**
